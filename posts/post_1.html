The tales of a relation called Siblings.

A journey mixed of Fights, Emotions, Sacrifices and much more.

Posted by Ayush Joshi on November 25, 2023

What is the first thing that comes to mind when you hear the word 'Siblings'? Fun? Gossips? Secrets? Fights? Well, keep this question at the back of your mind and answer it to yourself by the end of this article.

For me, it is something that always cheers me up when I feel low. Going down the memory lane could be incomplete without this statement.
We might fight a lot but the pampering after each fight is something which is always worth fighting.
We might irritate each other but when anyone troubles us, we be the defence to each other.
When things go wrong and we really need someone, we are the support to each other.

Time flew, we grew up, met many people, seen a lot of things including ups and downs and realised, the only thing that remained constant throughout this journey was this bond between us.

It is a permanent support system which is always there no matter what the situation is. I feel like from not sharing a single piece of chocolate to sharing everything about our lives we grow up, this bond grows up too.
So, What is the first thing which comes to mind when you hear the word 'Siblings'?
