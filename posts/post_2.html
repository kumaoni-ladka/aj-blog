Being Positive vs Being Optimistic.

The difference between "It will happen" vs "I hope it happens".

Posted by Ayush Joshi on January 08, 2024

I don't know how this topic springed to my mind. Perhaps I was forcing myself too much to write something or maybe I was trying to feel like a motivational speaker or it's because I've been in situations which made me think of it. Everyone has their respective opinion when it comes to being positive vs being Optimistic. So, I too thought to put down my opinion.

In my book, Being optimistic is like having hope or putting trust in something or someone. If I am expecting some help from you then I am being optimistic that I will be getting the help. It's like a belief I am having on you but being positive is a state of mind and in some cases we can even say it's the confidence. Be whatever the result would be, we accept it, take lessons, make notes, ponder on what went wrong and finally move on. That's more like being positive.

So, In short, If you're optimistic, you might not be positive all the time but if you learn to be positive, you will always come out of any sort of situations.
